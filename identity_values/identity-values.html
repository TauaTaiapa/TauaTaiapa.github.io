Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.

When my son turned 17, he really wanted to throw a birthday party with his friends at our house. The tricky part was that they wanted to have some drinks. After giving it some thought, I decided to allow the party with parental supervision. My reasoning was that I'd rather have them here where I could keep an eye on things, ensuring they were safe, rather than having them sneak off somewhere else. We made sure to provide both alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, along with plenty of food. Ultimately, everyone had a good time, and it was the right choice for everyone's safety and enjoyment.
